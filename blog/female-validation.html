the dangers of chasing female validation

When you chase validation, you change who you are just to be liked by someone who doesn't know you. You stop living for yourself and start living for approval.

If you crave her validation, you'll tolerate bad behavior just to keep her around. Respect yourself enough to walk away.

When your confidence depends on female attention, you're not in control of your emotions, someone else is.

You attract the wrong people. When you don't value yourself, you attract people who take advantage of that. Real connections are built on mutual respect, not desperation.

No amount of external validation will ever be enough if you don't believe in yourself first. Confidence comes from within

Focus on your purpose, build a life that makes you proud, and let validation be a bonus, not the goal my friend.
